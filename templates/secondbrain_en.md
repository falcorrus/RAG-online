## What are your favorite books and key takeaways from them?
Here's a brief list of books that have influenced me:
* **"Atomic Habits" (James Clear):** 1% changes every day yield colossal results over time. Focus on the system and processes, not on the end goals.
* **"How to Win Friends..." (Dale Carnegie):** Be genuinely interested in people. Remember the other person's name — it's the sweetest sound to them.
* **"Thinking, Fast and Slow" (Daniel Kahneman):** We have two systems of thinking. System 1 is fast, intuitive. System 2 is slow, logical. Learn to recognize the cognitive biases of System 1.

## Share quick recipes for delicious meals
Here are proven recipes:
### Carbonara Pasta (authentic)
* **Ingredients:** Spaghetti, guanciale (or bacon), egg yolks, Pecorino Romano cheese (or Parmesan), black pepper.
* **Preparation:** Fry the bacon. Cook pasta al dente. Mix yolks with grated cheese and pepper. Combine pasta with bacon, remove from heat, and quickly stir in the egg-cheese mixture, adding a little pasta cooking water for creaminess.

### Homemade Lemonade
* **Ingredients:** 3 lemons, 1 liter of water, 3-4 spoons of sugar/honey, mint.
* **Preparation:** Squeeze juice from lemons, mix with warm water and sugar until dissolved, chill, add mint and ice.

## What are your favorite places on Florianópolis Island (Magic Island)?
My personal recommendations on the Magic Island:
* **Barra da Lagoa Beach:** An excellent spot for beginner surfers. Be sure to visit the natural pools (Piscinas Naturais).
* **Lagoa da Conceição area:** The heart of the island's nightlife, many cafes with live music and great views of the lagoon.
* **Campeche Beach:** White sand, crystal clear water. From there, you can take a boat to Campeche Island (Ilha do Campeche) with ancient rock carvings.
